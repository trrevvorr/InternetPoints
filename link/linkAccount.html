To connect your device to the internet, try the following:

  1. On your Internet Points device, press and hold the button until the rear LED starts blinking blue (about 4 seconds)
  2. On your phone, go to the WiFi settings and connect to the network titled Photon-XXXXXX
  3. Click this link and follow the instructions
  4. After your Internet Points device is connected, the rear LED will start breathing cyan
  5. Scan the QR code again to finish setting up (this time your device will be connected)
